# BioTour

### JOURNEY INTO A SUSTAINABLE FUTURE.

Vegetable Oil Powered Bus - Solar Electricity

#### BioTour is a journey that aims to enliven the sustainability movement, while exploring the depths of America — the people, the land, and our cultures.

#### BioTour advocates sustainability as an essential goal of society and active democracy as a means to achieve it.
